Drone Operations

The Skymind V0 can fly for up to 30 minutes on a single charge. Most operational flights last around 15-25 minutes, with reserve time for safe return and landing. The system supports multiple flights per day with rapid recharging.

Recharging takes approximately 2x the flight time. After a 10-minute flight, it takes about 20 minutes to reach full charge. Our advanced charging system ensures the drone is ready for deployment quickly.

The Skymind V0 has a maximum operational radius of 10 kilometers (32,808 ft). Coverage depends on response time requirements and property layout. Multiple systems can be deployed for larger properties.

The system requires a pilot. Thanks to our remote flying capability, the pilot can be a remote piloting service. Regardless, a person must be on site to handle the drone and ensure safety.
