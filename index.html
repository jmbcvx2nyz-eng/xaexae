Drone Operations

The Skymind V0 can fly for up to 20 minutes on a single charge. Most operational flights last around 10-15 minutes, with reserve time for safe return and landing. The system supports multiple flights per day with rapid recharging.

Recharging takes approximately 2x the flight time. After a 10-minute flight, it takes about 20 minutes to reach full charge. Our advanced charging system ensures the drone is ready for deployment quickly.

The Skymind V0 has a maximum operational radius of approximately 500m (1,640 ft). Coverage depends on response time requirements and property layout. Multiple systems can be deployed for larger properties.

The system is fully autonomous, but most regions require a pilot-in-command (PiC) to be connected when the drone is flying. The PiC can be you, your security company, or a remote piloting service. The PiC primarily observes operations and intervenes only in emergencies.
